FIRST PAINTING |

My first painting in Acrylic. Was painted after my first diving experience in the Maldives. It took a month to complete.

THE SEA TURTLE |

First painting out of art School. The Green sea turtle in a reed of seaweed. This painting was done in Acrylic.

SHARK SCULPTURE |

During my days designing props in the theatre industry I fell in love with sculpture. This was made using a mixture of polyfill and white glue.

HARPER ILLUSTRATION |

My objective was to create an illustration using minimal realism in the style of Charley Harper. This was done by using the fewest possible visual elements while connecting all line work.

TRAVEL POSTER |

A minimalistic travel poster of Seychelles. Known for their giant tortoises and unique granite rock formations.

MAC BOOK COVER |

Design for a laptop cover. Dedicated to my favourite Boston Terrier pup, Bane.
