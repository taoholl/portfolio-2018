OBJECTIVE |

The objective was to create a branding identity for a theatre company. The theatre itself is known for its ivy vines encompassing the entire building. The brand was built around that visual que.

DESIGN |

The logo itself is made up of the visual representation of the Ivy vine, the Iconic theatre tragedy mask and the stage lights. The logo is simplistic and modern while still staying true to the authenticity of the theatre itself.
