PROBLEM STATEMENT

Traveler’s need a flexible, reliable guide and itinerary when exploring new places because they want to experience an organized, safe and fulfilling trip.

DEFINITION STATEMENT

Vacay is a custom itinerary creator that allows travelers to be more organized, reducing stress and frustration.

STARTING YOUR VACAY

Start by creating a new Vacation by selecting your vacation style. Then watch Vacay create your custom itinerary by submitting your confirmation emails.

DASHBOARD NAVIAGATION

Navigate through your new dashboard. Check weather updates in your destination and allow Vacay to suggest activities to book while you are on your vacation.

ITINERARY LAYOUT

Vacay allows you to view your itinerary is numerous ways, all shareable to people with the vacay app.An interactive experience where your loved ones can literally see your progress. A Step by step break down and a printable version.
