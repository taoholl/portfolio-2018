PROJECT OBJECTIVE

The project objective was to create a motion piece using handmade, tactile assets. The project was open concept.

SKETCHES

My idea started off very elaborate, telling the story of a man and a stray, and how recently the mans heart was broken and he thought everything was lost. The man is seen in a park and buries himself in a self help book. A lonely dog notices that the man is upset and tried everything to make it better by bringing him his most beloved things found in the park. It is only at the end the story when a ball hits the mans foot that he becomes aware of the world around him, and sees that there is kindness everywhere, and this dog, who did not know him, loves him.

The project would be done in stop motion using clay and a handbuilt set.

PROBLEMS

In reality I did not have the experience or the time to do this project. This was one of the first motion projects we did and was my first attempt at stop motion. The clay I bought was cheap, and although it said that it was not quick dry, it dried almost immediately which meant I had to cut the story short and move quickly. I immediately changed my aesthetic to a grungy world.

THE EVOLUTION

Because of my time restrictions and the clay cracked easily, I created a world where everything was grungy.
